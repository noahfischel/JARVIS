===============================================================================
:: Email Module
===============================================================================
							Created by Noah Fischel

For all your messaging needs. It checks your unread emails, reads the subject
and now; with a brand new function: AUTOCHECK! Turn the autocheck on and you'll
be warned when a new message arrives!!! Is it awesome or WHAT?!?!?!
Obs: Works for Gmail ONLY!


===============================================================================
:: Command List
===============================================================================

>> Open email

>> Turn email autocheck ON/OFF
>> check email autocheck

>> Check/Read email
>> Stop Reading

>> Change email Username
>> Change email Password

>> Change email limit

===============================================================================
:: Command Analysis
===============================================================================

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
>> Open Email

EX:	Open my Email
	Show me my emails

Simply opens gmail
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
>> Turn email autocheck ON/OFF

EX:	Turn email autocheck ON/OFF
	Initiate email autocheck, please
	Terminate email autocheck

THE BRAND NEW AUTOCHECK! While ON it will check your email every X minutes (set
the value of the intervals in the database) for received messages. It doesnt
check your UNREAD emails, but the ones you RECEIVE! Eg: You have 5 unread
emails and you receive 2 new messages while autocheck is ON, so you will be
informed of: YOU RECEIVED 2 NEW MESSAGES! Turn OFF when needed.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
>> Check email autocheck

EX:	Check email autocheck, would you?
	Is email autocheck ON?
	Can you check if is email autocheck OFF?

Checks wether email autocheck is ON or OFF.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
>> Check/Read email

Ex:	Check my email
	Do I have new emails?
	How many unread emails have I got?
	Can you please read my email?

Checks your email for unread messages. If you do have them, it asks for
confirmation to read the subjects for you. A simple "yes, please" or "no, thank
you" answers it.
If asked to read your email it skips the confirmation part altogether.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
>> Stop Reading

Ex:	Stop Reading please
	Stop Talking
	Thank you
	That's enough

Stops reading your emails, in case there are too many of them.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
>> Change Username|Password

Set Gmail username|password
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
>> Change email limit

Changes the maximum amount of emails read. Set to 0 for ALL (not advised)
